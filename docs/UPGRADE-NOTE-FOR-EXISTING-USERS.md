## We've Upgraded VibeOS — Here's How to Get It

If you installed VibeOS in your project before, you're on the original version. We've added a lot: TDD enforcement, better Work Order governance, and a simple upgrade flow. Here's how to get it.

### What to Do (30 seconds)

1. Open your project in Claude Code, Cursor, or Codex.
2. Say this to your agent:

   **"Upgrade VibeOS from https://github.com/chieflatif/VibeOS-2"**

3. That's it. The agent fetches the latest from GitHub, updates your scripts and gates, and tells you what's new. Your baselines and config stay as-is. You don't need the repo cloned — the agent handles it.

### What Changes

- **Updated:** All gate scripts, new gates (including TDD enforcement)
- **Preserved:** Your known baselines, env config, WO-INDEX, DEVELOPMENT-PLAN
